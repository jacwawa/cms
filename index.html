How To Use This Guide

  1. Start with Quickstart.
  2. Follow Core Workflows for common tasks.
  3. Use Troubleshooting if you hit errors.
  4. Copy approved templates from Snippets and Reference.
  5. Run Launch Checklist before publishing.

Fountain Areas You Will Use Most

  • Shows: create and maintain show pages
  • Assets: upload images and PDF files
  • Collections: home page, series pages, most pages other than shows
  • Events: auto-generated instances tied to show dates
  • CMS: San Juan Hill and Press content area

Keep new content Invisible until approved and use relative URLs where directed.
